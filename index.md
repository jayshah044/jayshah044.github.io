# 🌿 leaf-venation - Visualize Leaf Patterns Simply

## 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the leaf-venation project! Here, you can easily visualize leaf venation patterns based on the research paper titled "Modeling and visualization of leaf venation patterns." This application aims to provide a user-friendly experience for anyone interested in the fascinating designs of leaves.

- **Easy Visualization:** Quickly create visual representations of leaf venation patterns.

## ❓ How to Use the Application

1. **Open the Application**
   Launch the leaf-venation software from your device.

2. **Upload Leaf Images**
   Import images of leaves you wish to analyze. The application supports common image formats like JPEG and PNG.

3. **Analyze Your Leaf**
   Once uploaded, the application will automatically process the image and display the venation pattern.

4. **Explore Visualization Options**
   Use various tools provided to adjust the visualization, such as changing colors or adding annotations.

5. **Save Your Work**
   Export your visualizations using the "Save" option, and choose the desired format.
